How Connecto protects your privacy

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Velyzo, your privacy is fundamental to everything we do. Connecto is designed with privacy at its core, ensuring that your network requests and personal information remain secure and private.

Information We Collect

Connecto is designed to minimize data collection. Here's what we do and don't collect:

What We DON'T Collect:

  • Your network requests or responses
  • API endpoints, URLs, or server addresses you access
  • Request headers, parameters, or body content
  • Authentication tokens or credentials
  • Personal identifiers or contact information
  • Location data or device identifiers

What We MAY Collect:

  • Crash reports to improve app stability (anonymized)
  • Basic usage analytics (which features are used, not what data is processed)
  • App performance metrics to optimize user experience

Data Storage and Processing

All your network configurations, saved requests, and app settings are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it through system features like iCloud sync or AirDrop.

iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync, your app preferences and saved requests will be synchronized across your Apple devices through Apple's iCloud service. This data is encrypted and handled according to Apple's privacy standards. You can disable this feature at any time in the app settings.

Third-Party Services

Connecto does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. All network requests you make are direct from your device to your specified endpoints.

Apple Watch Data

Data synchronized between your iPhone and Apple Watch (such as saved requests and preferences) remains within the Apple ecosystem and is protected by Apple's security measures.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is encrypted on your device
  • Network communications use secure protocols when available
  • No server-side storage of your personal network requests
  • Regular security audits and updates

Your Rights

Since most data never leaves your device, you have complete control:

  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the app
  • Disable iCloud sync in app settings
  • Reset all configurations within the app
  • Contact us for any privacy-related questions

Children's Privacy

Connecto is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app and updating the "Last updated" date below.
