How Connecto protects your privacy

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Velyzo, your privacy is fundamental to everything we do. Connecto is designed with privacy at its core as a completely offline application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your data.

Information We Collect

Connecto is designed as a completely offline application. Here's what we do and don't collect:

What We DON'T Collect:

  • Your network requests or responses
  • API endpoints, URLs, or server addresses you access
  • Request headers, parameters, or body content
  • Authentication tokens or credentials
  • Personal identifiers or contact information
  • Location data or device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or telemetry data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic information
  • Any data whatsoever - the app operates completely offline

What We Collect:

  • Nothing. Connecto does not connect to any servers owned or operated by Velyzo.
  • No analytics. We do not use any analytics services or tracking.
  • No crash reporting. We do not collect crash reports or diagnostic data.

Data Storage and Processing

All your network configurations, saved requests, and app settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage mechanisms. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any external servers, including our own.

No Server Communication

Connecto operates as a completely offline application:

  • No Velyzo servers: The app does not communicate with any servers owned or operated by Velyzo
  • No third-party services: We do not use any analytics, crash reporting, or tracking services
  • Direct network requests only: All network requests you make go directly from your device to your specified endpoints
  • No data transmission: Your configurations and request history never leave your device

iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync in your device settings, your app preferences and saved requests may be synchronized across your Apple devices through Apple's iCloud service. Important:

  • This sync happens directly between your devices via Apple's iCloud
  • Velyzo does not have access to your iCloud data
  • We do not process or store any iCloud-synced data on our servers
  • This data is encrypted and handled according to Apple's privacy standards
  • You can disable iCloud sync at any time in your device settings

Third-Party Services

Connecto does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data collection services. The app operates completely independently without any external dependencies for data processing or storage.

Apple Watch Data

Data synchronized between your iPhone and Apple Watch (such as saved requests and preferences) remains within the Apple ecosystem and is protected by Apple's security measures.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, your privacy is protected by Apple's built-in security measures:

  • All data is encrypted on your device using Apple's security framework
  • Network communications use secure protocols when available (HTTPS)
  • No server-side storage means no risk of data breaches from our systems
  • App data is protected by your device's passcode, Touch ID, or Face ID
  • No network vulnerabilities related to data transmission to our servers

Your Rights

Since all data remains on your device, you have complete control:

  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the app
  • Clear individual requests and configurations within the app
  • Control iCloud sync through your device settings
  • No need to request data deletion from us - we don't have any of your data
  • Contact us for any privacy-related questions

Children's Privacy

Connecto is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app and updating the "Last updated" date below.
